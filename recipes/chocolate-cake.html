Chocolate Cake

Heavn is in your kitchen!

Addicted! That's what you'll say once you bite into this mouthwatering soft chocolate cake that uses no milk!

Ingredients

    Cake
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup oil or butter
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder (50%+ cocoa)
  • 1 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 cups wheat flour

    • Icing
  • 395g condensed milk
  • 200g thick cream
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cocoa powder (50%+)

Method

    Cake
  1. Preheat oven to 170 degrees.
  2. Pour eggs in bowl and which for 1 minute. Add sugar and oil, mix for 2 minutes.
  3. Add cocoa powder and boiled water, mix for 1 minute.
  4. In separate bowl, add baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, siff in flour, mix then add into liquid mixture.
  5. Coat cake pan with enough butter, then flour. Pour dough in cake pan and bake until cooked (30 minutes).
  6. Once baked, allow to cool down.

    7. Icing
  7. Pour condensed milk in pot on low heat.
  8. Add cream, butter, cocoa powder, cotinuously mix with whisk until thick and heavy (pot may burn a bit).
  9. Slowly and strategically pour the icing over the top of the cake while smoothing over with a baking spatula.
  10. Sprinkle toasted almond pieces or shredded coconut to decorate.

    11. You desserve this treat!
