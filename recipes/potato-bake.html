Potato Bake

More cheese!

Finally a homemade dish that tastes like restaurant food. A potato bake so cheesy you'll want seconds.

Ingredients

  • 8 medium size dpotatoes
  • 300ml milk
  • 200ml fresh cream
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 150g bacon bits
  • 250g mozarella, 250g cheddar

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Peel and cut potatoes into thin discs.
  3. Boil in pot for 5 minutes, with a pinch of salt and butter (must not become too soft). Drain and place aside.
  4. In a frying pan, sautee onions and cropped gralic in very little oil, fry bacon bits until cooked.
  5. In a saucepan on medium heat,add butter, let melt, add cornflour and whisk until it creates a paste.
  6. Slowly add cream while continuously stiring, allow to thicken.
  7. Slowly add milk while continuously stiring, allow to thicken.
  8. Add black pepper, nutmeg and a pinch of salt.
  9. Add 100g of cheddar cheese, stir then remove from stove.
  10. In a baking dish, thinly layer potatoes to cover surface of the dish.
  11. Add layer of the cheese sauce, sprinkle all the bacon bits.
  12. Place second layer of potatoes to cover, sprinkle 200g of cheddar and mozarella cheese over the top.
  13. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  14. Remove foil, sprinkle more cheese and bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is golden.
  15. Garnish with thyme (optional), allow to cool for 5-10 minutes before serving.

    16. Dinner is served!
