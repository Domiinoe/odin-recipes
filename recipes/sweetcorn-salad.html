Sweetcorn Salad

Description

This salad packs a punch , you'll want to have as your main lunch and not a starter.

With only seven ingredients, it's fresh, quick to make and easy to customise.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 2 sweetcorn cobs
  • 1/2 shredded cabbage
  • 3 avocados
  • Lettuce
  • Soybean sprouts

Steps

  1. Place brown rice in some water and rince out. Add rice into pot, pour 2 cups of water with some salt and oil.
  2. Cover with lid and let cook on medium heat for 5 minutes, reduce to low heat and let cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Once cooked, place onto separate plates and allow to cool. This will be your base.
  4. Steam the sweetcorn and cabbage with a pinch of salt for about 7 minutes, let cool then remove the corn from the cob.
  5. Wash lettuce, tomatoes and soybeans. Cut up tomatoes, avocado and lettuce -if necessary into smaller pieces.
  6. On top of your plates of brown rice, in sections: add the cherry tomatoes, sweetcorn, cabbage, soybeans and lettuce.
  7. Add a generous amount of avocado.
  8. Serve with ranch salad dressing or one of your choice.

    9. Enjoy!
